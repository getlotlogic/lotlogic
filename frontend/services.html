02TRIPWIRE

Tripwire — eyes at every entrance.

If your lot has a way in and a way out, Tripwire watches both. Every plate that crosses the line gets read. Permanent plates are recognized. Temporary passes register in thirty seconds. Anything else trips the wire — and gets handled. Automatically, with the photo attached.

/ what this prevents

Strangers in the lot. Endless phone calls. Hours of staff time lost to parking every week.

  • The "someone's in my spot again" call
  • Sticker chaos and hangtag printing

  • Phantom drivers who never leave
/ how we do it

We install solar-powered cameras at the entrances. No wiring, no permits we can't pull, no monthly fees. Permanent and Temporary plates both register via QR — same flow, different lifespan.

Tripwire handles the rest. A quiet 15-minute grace window for anyone unregistered. After that — dispatched.

Zero stickers. Zero hangtags. Zero phone calls. Zero new software for your team to learn. The system runs without anyone touching it. Approve us once — we keep running.

Property managers shouldn't be parking enforcers. You have a thousand other things going on. We take the parking off your plate. Completely.

Cars slipping in past a single gate. Coverage gaps on properties without a chokepoint. Vehicles moving spots to avoid notice.
