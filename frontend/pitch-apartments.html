/ automated ticketing

Enforcement without a tow.

Not every violation needs a tow truck. For first-time strangers, brief overstays, or properties where escalation should be measured, our automated ticketing tier issues a citation the moment a vehicle's grace window expires — with photo evidence and full audit trail.

●

15-minute grace window.

Every new vehicle gets a quiet 15 minutes to register via QR. Plenty of time for legitimate stays, almost zero patience for drivers who keep coming back.

●

Citation issued automatically.

If grace expires and the camera confirms the vehicle is still parked, a ticket is issued — timestamped, plate-photographed, geo-tagged.

●

Defensible photo evidence.

Each ticket includes the entry photo, a confirmation from the patrol cycle, and the registration window timeline. Disputes resolve in seconds.

●

Permanent & Temporary plates ignored.

Permanent plates, employees, and Drivers with active passes never trigger a citation. The system only acts on vehicles that don't belong.

●

Drive-throughs auto-closed.

Pulled in to turn around or use the bathroom? If the vehicle exits before grace ends, the session closes silently. No false tickets.

●

Optional escalation to tow.

Set the rules per property. Repeat offenders, fire-lane parking, or unpaid citation thresholds can auto-escalate to your tow partner.
