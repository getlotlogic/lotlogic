/ static cameras

The camera does the patrol.

Our cameras are fixed-mount, not vehicle-mounted. They live at the lot 24/7, watch every vehicle that enters, and read plates onboard in real time. You never drive a patrol — the alert comes only after a violation is already documented.

●

Always on, never out of position.

Vehicle-mounted cameras miss everything when the truck is parked or driving elsewhere. A static camera doesn't blink — every vehicle, every minute, every lot.

●

Onboard plate recognition.

Plate text is read on the camera itself in under a second. No round-trip to a cloud OCR vendor, no delay between "vehicle entered" and "plate identified."

●

PTZ for the whole lot.

Pan-tilt-zoom cameras patrol parking rows on a schedule and lock onto vehicles as they enter. One camera does what would take three fixed bullets.

●

Buffered when offline.

If the network drops, the camera holds events on local storage and re-transmits when it reconnects. Nothing dropped during outages.

●

Onboard whitelist.

Permanent and active Temporary plates live on the camera itself. Enforcement is local — no false alerts on registered vehicles, even if our servers blink.

●

Photo evidence on every alert.

Plate crop + wide context shot + timestamp + GPS-tagged location. The evidence pack is built before the SMS hits your phone.

/ how it works

We handle everything upstream.

You handle the tow.

1

Partner properties get a solar-powered camera at their entrance at no cost. That's the hook — and it means they say yes fast.

2

Every plate at every partner property gets registered in 30 seconds. The system knows who belongs and who doesn't.

3

You get SMS alerts the moment a violation happens.

The second an unregistered vehicle overstays, the alert lands on your phone. Plate, vehicle, location, photo. Documented proof.

4

You dispatch a truck. You collect the fee.

No patrols, no guessing, no "the property manager called us" delays. Every tow is pre-verified before you drive out.
